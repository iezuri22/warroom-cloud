One-time setup
For the button to actually flip Focus on your devices, you need a Shortcut named Deep Work on your iPhone + Mac, with iCloud sync on.
Show setup steps
  1. iPhone: Settings → Focus → + → create "Deep Work"; pick allowed apps.
  2. iPhone: Settings → Focus → Share Across Devices → on.
  3. Mac: System Settings → Focus → Share across devices → on. Configure Deep Work allowed apps for Mac.
  4. iPhone: Shortcuts app → + → action "Set Focus → Deep Work → Turn On → For [duration]". Name shortcut exactly Deep Work.
  5. Shortcuts app → Settings → iCloud Sync → on. The shortcut now appears on your Mac too.
